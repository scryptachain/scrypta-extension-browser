How it works

Connect to Scrypta Ecosystem directly from your browser

1. Activate the plugin

You'll find it in the upper right-hand corner of your browser. One click and it's active.

2. Create new identity

Write your name or a cool nickname. A strong password is recommended.

3. That's it!

You already have your account. Now you can access the dApps from here.
